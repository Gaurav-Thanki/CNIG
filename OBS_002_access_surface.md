# Observation 002 — Access expands through connection

A system had clear and correct access rules.

Each rule worked in isolation.

Nothing was incorrect.

---

But when systems were connected, access appeared in places it was not explicitly defined.

Not through violation.

Through composition.

---

## What changed

Access did not increase in rules.

It increased in paths.

---

## CNIG lens (informal)

Structure creates new reachable access patterns without changing permissions.

---

## Primitive that feels relevant

Privilege Surface

Access behaves like something that emerges from connectivity.

---

## Invariant feeling

Structural Invariant remains locally intact but globally stretched.
