# Observation 004 — Identity changes under aggregation

Identity systems behaved correctly at all individual levels.

Users, roles, and policies were valid.

---

But when aggregated (groups + nested roles + inherited permissions), identity became inconsistent across views.

---

## What changed

Identity remained correct.

But it was no longer singular.

---

## CNIG lens (informal)

Composition turns identity into a structural effect, not a fixed object.

---

## Primitive that feels relevant

Reachable State Space

Identity depends on what configurations are reachable through aggregation.

---

## Invariant feeling

Identity Invariant appears stable in parts, unstable in combination.
