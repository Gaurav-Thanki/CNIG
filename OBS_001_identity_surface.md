# Observation 001 — Identity does not stay local

This was seen in a system where identities were correct at the individual level.

Users, roles, and groups all behaved as expected.

Nothing was wrong locally.

---

But when the system was viewed through composition (groups + roles + nested access), identity started behaving differently.

The same identity did not feel consistent across contexts.

---

## What changed

Identity did not break.

Its meaning shifted depending on where it was observed.

---

## CNIG lens (informal)

Identity becomes dependent on structure once systems are composed.

---

## Primitive that feels relevant

### Reachable State Space
Identity appears to depend on what contexts can reach it.

---

## Invariant feeling

Identity Invariant feels locally stable but globally inconsistent.
