# Observation 006 — Same pipeline, different outcomes

A CI/CD pipeline behaved consistently in design.

Each step was valid.

---

But across environments, final outcomes differed.

Not due to failure in steps.

But due to composition of environments.

---

## What changed

Same process.
Different system behavior.

---

## CNIG lens (informal)

System structure changes meaning of execution.

---

## Primitive that feels relevant

Execution vs Governance Separation

Execution is stable, interpretation is not.

---

## Invariant feeling

Stability varies across deployment contexts.
